NOTE 30 VIP

The Infinix NOTE 30 VIP is a mid-range smartphone offering from Infinix that runs on a MediaTek Dimensity 8050 chipset with 8GB of RAM, Android 13 OS, and the XOS 13 interface.

The Infinix NOTE 30 VIP features a 6.67-inch FHD+ AMOLED display with a 120Hz screen refresh rate, 108MP + 2MP + 2MP triple rear cameras, and a 32 Megapixel selfie camera. There’s also an under-display fingerprint scanner and 256GB of expandable storage.

A large 5,000mAh battery powers the device with 68W fast charging technology. The Infinix NOTE 30 VIP is predicted to be priced for around ₱13,999 (12 + 256GB).
